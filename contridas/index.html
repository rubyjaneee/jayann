Planning and Application of Arduino Based Accident Alert System

The primary purpose of an Arduino-Based Accident Alert System aims to increase vehicle accident detection and reporting to improve emergency response and road safety. It is intended to detect accidents automatically, assess their seriousness, and notify the appropriate authorities and emergency services. Minimizing response times, lowering accident-related mortality, and helping accident victims are important goals.

  • It automatically detects the accident and based on that information it sends that location to the hospital, relatives, and to the police quickly.

  • Low cost.

  • Easy to implement, easy to use.

  • Processing speed.

  • High accuracy, and its self-reliance.

Ec-claw Machine

Combining a claw machine with waste segregation can be an innovative way to promote recycling and environmental awareness. You could design a game where players use the claw to pick up and sort different types of waste into separate bins, earning points for correctly segregating items. This could encourage responsible disposal practices while making the process fun and engaging.

  • Fun but resourceful

  • A unique idea to pursuade individuals to care for the nature.

  • It comes with a prize.

  • Bottles will serve as a token.

  • A game that would help mother nature.
